About

Capitol Contracts LLC develops plain-language educational resources that help staff, advocates, and program partners translate survival responses into safer communication, clearer accountability, and usable next steps.

Current Focus

The Rescue Boat: Survival Doctrine

A peer-written educational framework translating trauma, addiction, shame, and freeze response into practical concepts for institutional and court-program use.

The staff task is not to affirm the behavior. The task is to identify the function, name the damage, and redirect toward safer tools.

What Really Happened / WRH

A structured educational curriculum architecture designed to make complex nervous-system and survival-pattern concepts understandable without requiring trauma disclosure.
