The Rescue Boat: Survival Doctrine

For the person called noncompliant when they are frozen.

A peer-written resource for institutional staff who need practical communication tools and participants who need language without shame, excuses, or forced disclosure.

The staff task is not to affirm the behavior. The task is to identify the function, name the damage, and redirect toward safer tools.
Open the Resource Hub Read the Participant Edition

For programs, this is a field-ready way to talk about freeze, shame, shutdown, and accountability without turning explanation into excuse. For participants, this is a plain-language map for what happens when the body locks, the shame hits, and the next step feels impossible.
