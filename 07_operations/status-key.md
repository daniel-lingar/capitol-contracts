# Status Key

## Draft
Material exists but is not ready to send.

## Working
Material is being actively revised or assembled.

## Internal-Ready
Safe for internal use, but not for clients.

## Client-Ready
Safe to send to outside agencies, buyers, partners, or reviewers.

## Public-Ready
Safe to publish on the website or GitHub Pages.

## Needs Review
Needs legal, business, factual, or executive review before use.

## Archived
Do not use unless restoring old language for reference.

## Dead
Obsolete, replaced, or unsafe to reuse.
