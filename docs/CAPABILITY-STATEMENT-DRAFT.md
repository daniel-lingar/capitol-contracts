## Company Overview

Capitol Contracts LLC develops structured, non-clinical psychoeducational education materials, implementation tools, and training frameworks for organizations serving adults in recovery, reentry, veteran-support, peer-support, housing-stabilization, and community-based support settings.

The company focuses on plain-language pattern-recognition education, facilitator-ready curriculum materials, participant support tools, and implementation documentation that can operate alongside existing clinical, peer, case-management, recovery, or community-support services.

Capitol Contracts LLC materials are **not therapy**, **not medical advice**, **not legal advice**, **not crisis care**, and **not a replacement for licensed professional support**.

## Primary Program: What Really Happened (WRH)

**What Really Happened (WRH)** is a structured non-clinical psychoeducational curriculum system focused on helping participants understand survival patterns, nervous-system adaptation, executive-function disruption, shame cycles, freeze responses, avoidance loops, and practical interruption points.

WRH uses a non-exposure model. Participants do not have to disclose personal trauma histories to engage the material.

## Differentiators

- **Non-disclosure design:** participants can learn the pattern without being required to share personal history.
- **Mechanism-first education:** behavior is translated by function and sequence, not identity-level labels.
- **Facilitator-ready structure:** materials are organized for consistent delivery and documentation.
- **Plain-language approach:** complex concepts are translated into accessible educational tools.
- **Safety-boundary orientation:** materials include non-clinical scope language, participant pause/exit tools, and referral boundaries for needs outside program scope.
